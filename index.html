The Restaurant

A New Vision for
Mexican Dining

Corazon De Leon is a phenomenal new restaurant that has quickly captured the hearts of the Santa Cruz Mountains community. Located just off Highway 9 in Ben Lomond, this family restaurant brings fresh, vibrant Mexican cuisine to the redwoods.

Every ingredient is sourced with intention. From the first bite, guests are transported by the freshness and authenticity of each dish — a culinary experience that leaves diners speechless and eager to return.

♪ Live Mariachi Entertainment

Celebrate special occasions in style with live Mariachi music — an unforgettable experience perfect for birthdays, family gatherings, and group dinners.
